Three days without water and the debate is over.

Off-grid water purification covers the methods used to make questionable water safe to drink without municipal utilities — including filtration, boiling, chemical treatment, and safe storage. GridDownData stores these references offline on an air-gapped USB drive for use during outages or in remote areas.
