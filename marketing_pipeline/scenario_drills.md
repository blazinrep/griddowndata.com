# Scenario of the Month — 6-Month Drill Calendar

Use this template to publish one grid-down scenario drill per month. Each scenario should drive traffic to a specific vault section, build email list engagement, and reinforce the product's offline value.

---

## Month 1 — Grid-Down Water Contamination

- **Trigger event:** Boil-water advisory, flooding, or municipal treatment failure.
- **Scenario:** A three-day contamination event has made tap water unsafe. Cell towers are overloaded. You cannot search online.
- **User action:** Use the vault to find the correct water purification protocol, dosage, and storage steps for their household size.
- **Vault resources:** `02_WATER_SANITATION/EPA_Emergency_Water_Disinfection.pdf`
- **Call to action:** "If you had to purify water tonight without a search engine, would you know the exact dose?"
- **Content format:** Blog post + short video walkthrough + email checklist.

---

## Month 2 — Regional Mapping Navigation Exercise

- **Trigger event:** GPS outage, road closure, or evacuation route confusion.
- **Scenario:** You need to reach a family rally point 40 miles away using only paper maps and terrain features. Phone navigation is down.
- **User action:** Plot a route, identify two alternate paths, and list three water sources along the way.
- **Vault resources:** `Offline Navigation` section — topographic maps and compass/GPS fallback guides.
- **Call to action:** "When the blue dot disappears, the person with a map still knows where to go."
- **Content format:** PDF exercise worksheet + community challenge with photo submissions.

---

## Month 3 — Solar Power Deficit Calculator Drill

- **Trigger event:** Rolling blackouts, winter storm, or generator failure.
- **Scenario:** Your battery bank is at 40% and you must prioritize devices for the next 48 hours.
- **User action:** Calculate daily watt-hour load, decide what to keep running, and identify which non-electric backups to deploy.
- **Vault resources:** `04_MECHANICAL_TOOLS_ENERGY/Off_Grid_Solar_Wind.pdf`
- **Call to action:** "Watts are a budget. Do you know your monthly average down to the hour?"
- **Content format:** Calculator spreadsheet download + guided worksheet.

---

## Month 4 — No-Doctor Wound Care Response

- **Trigger event:** Remote injury during a blackout, storm, or supply-chain disruption.
- **Scenario:** A family member has a deep laceration. EMS is 90 minutes away. You must stabilize, control bleeding, and decide on evacuation versus shelter-in-place.
- **User action:** Follow the wound-care protocol in the vault and assemble a field trauma kit from household items.
- **Vault resources:** `01_MEDICAL_FIRST_AID/Emergency_Medicine_Manual.pdf`
- **Call to action:** "The first hour after injury is owned by whoever is standing there. Will that be you?"
- **Content format:** Step-by-step infographic + email sequence.

---

## Month 5 — Long-Term Food Storage Audit

- **Trigger event:** Rising food prices, regional crop failure, or trucking disruption.
- **Scenario:** You want to ensure your household can eat for 90 days without resupply. You have limited freezer space and no generator.
- **User action:** Inventory current stores, identify gaps, and build a 30-day dry/can rotation plan using the vault guides.
- **Vault resources:** `03_FOOD_PRESERVATION_HOMESTEADING/USDA_Canning_1930.pdf`
- **Call to action:** "A full pantry is comfort. A documented rotation plan is preparedness."
- **Content format:** Printable audit checklist + before/after challenge.

---

## Month 6 — Communications & Local Network Build

- **Trigger event:** Internet/cell outage, social unrest, or regional emergency.
- **Scenario:** You need to establish reliable local communication with neighbors and family within a 5-mile radius.
- **User action:** Document local contacts, choose backup comms methods, and define message schedules.
- **Vault resources:** Cross-reference `Survival Wikis` and `Infrastructure Guides` for comms protocols.
- **Call to action:** "The strongest grid-down asset is the network you build before the outage."
- **Content format:** Community template + downloadable neighborhood contact sheet.
